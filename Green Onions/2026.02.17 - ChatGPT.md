## **Question prompts:**

what is the ideal temperature to germinate green onions?

## **ChatGPT Response:**

Green onions (scallions) germinate best in cool-to-moderate soil temperatures.

Ideal temperature range:

🌱 55–75°F (13–24°C) is the sweet spot

Optimal: around 65–70°F (18–21°C)

A few extra tips that help:

They’ll still germinate in cooler soil (down to ~45°F / 7°C), but it’ll be slower.

Temperatures much above 80°F (27°C) can reduce germination success.

Keep the soil consistently moist, not soggy—drying out is the #1 germination killer.

If you’re starting them indoors, a heat mat usually isn’t necessary—room temp is perfect. Outdoors, early spring or fall conditions are ideal.
